Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add books to my library?

You can add books by scanning the barcode using the camera feature, or by searching for books using the search function.

How do I borrow a book from a friend?

Navigate to your friend's library, find the book you want, and tap "Request Exchange". Your friend will receive a notification and can approve or reject the request.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account through the app settings. This will permanently remove all your data including books, reviews, and connections.

How do I change my reading status for a book?

Open the book details page and select your reading status from the options: "Want to Read", "Currently Reading", or "Finished".

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All passwords are encrypted, and we use secure connections (HTTPS) for all data transmission. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection to sync data with our servers. However, you can view previously loaded content when offline.
