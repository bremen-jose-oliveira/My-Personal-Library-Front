Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

My Personal Library ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register and use our app, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Information from third-party authentication providers (Google, Apple) when you choose to sign in with these services

2.2 Library Data

To provide our core services, we collect:

  • Book Information: Books you add to your library, including titles, authors, ISBNs, and book covers
  • Reading Status: Your reading progress and status for each book
  • Reviews: Reviews and ratings you write for books
  • Exchange Information: Book exchange requests, borrow/loan history

2.3 Social Features

When using social features, we collect:

  • Friendship Data: Friend connections and friend requests
  • Notifications: Notifications about exchanges, friend requests, and reviews

2.4 Device Information

We may collect device-specific information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Camera access (for barcode scanning)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and manage your book library and reading lists
  • Facilitate book exchanges between users
  • Enable social features like friend connections and reviews
  • Send you notifications about app activity
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Authentication Providers

We use the following third-party authentication services:

  • Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, Google's Privacy Policy applies to the data they collect. You can review it at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple Sign-In: When you sign in with Apple, Apple's Privacy Policy applies. You can review it at: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4.2 Book Data

We may use Google Books API to fetch book information. Google's Privacy Policy applies to their services.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored on secure servers
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • We use HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, profile information, and public book library may be visible to other users when you use social features
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information through the app settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications or limit data sharing through app settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
